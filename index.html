Plenty of Furballs

About 6 million companion animals enter animal shelters nationwide every year. Of those, approximately 3 million are dogs and 3 million are cats. Please think twice before you purchase your pet from a breeder.

Happy & Healthy

Many shelters provide a health screen that includes vaccinations, parasite treatment, and spay or neuter surgery that is covered in the adoption fee. If services are not provided, a free veterinary visit often is included in the adoption fee.

Take Your Pick

Organizations like animal shelters, humane societies, and rescue leagues will house and care for abandoned, stray and injured animals. They may also rehabilitate abused animals to help them recover and find a loving home.
