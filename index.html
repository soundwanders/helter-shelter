Plenty of Furballs

About 6 million companion animals enter animal shelters nationwide every year. Of those, approximately 3 million are dogs and 3 million are cats.

Please reconsider purchasing your pets from a retail store or breeder.

Happy & Healthy

Many shelters provide a full health screen that includes vaccinations, parasite treatment, and spay/neuter surgery, all covered in the adoption fee.

If services are not provided, a free veterinary visit often is included in the adoption fee.

No Pet Left Behind

Organizations like animal shelters, humane societies, and rescue leagues will house and care for owner-surrendered, abandoned, stray and injured animals.

Many organizations also rehabilitate abused animals to help them recover and find a loving home.
