Adopt, Don't Shop!

Animal shelters have plenty of pets of all shapes and sizes. Whether you want to raise a puppy or an old buddy to hang on the couch with you, your local shelter can make it happen.

Vaccination is an important component of any shelter or animal rescue organization's overall health management program. Every shelter must develop a vaccination protocol that is tailored to their current animal population's needs.
The Shelter Map contains pinned locations of New Hampshire Animal Shelters. Shelters which euthanize animals are not pinned, so this map is no-kill shelters only! Check out the map below, which shelter are you closest to?
Some shelters are open-intake, meaning they will accept any stray or abandoned animals, no questions asked. If you are unable to care for your pet, contact a local organization to make sure your animal is able to find another loving home.
